How do I join the queue?

Type '!pixels add your_gamer_tag' (ie. !pixels add demonbae#8173) into Twitch chat.

How does the queue work?

The queue priority is customized by the streamer. Subscriber status and loyalty points could be factors in your place in line. Even if you join first, someone with higher priority could pass your place in line.
